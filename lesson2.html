Bible Texts: Psalms 1:2-3 Proverbs 13:20

2. Meditation and Reflection: Phil. 4:8; Joshua 1:8; Psalms 143:5; 1Tim. 4:15
Meditation is a practice that involves focusing or clearing your mind using a combination of mental, spiritual and physical techniques. Focus, attention, solitude, silence and concentration are critical essentials for meditation. Contemplation and reflection.

During meditation and reflection, you take notes of insights, inspiration, ideas, instructions, urgings, promptings, directions etc., received so as to enable you make use of them for your progress and advancement. The prodigal son, when at cross-roads, used meditation, contemplation and reflection to advance his life forward - Luke 15:17-19

3. Surround yourself with positive influencers: Prov. 13:20
This will affect every aspect of your life, career, academic pursuits, marriage, family life, health, business, work, spirituality, personal growth, social life, morality, your net worth, values, beliefs, interest etc.

Hang around or associate with positive influencers and people you admire to be like or achieve what they did. Disconnect from toxic people and those influencing you negatively. Cut them out of your life. Gen. 21:10-12; Matt. 5:29-30 Audit your relationship and life regularly. Isaiah 6:5

Ask yourself these Questions:
Who are those surrounding me?
What are they doing to me?
Are they substractors, dividers, aiders or multipliers?
Practice expanded or restricted relationships.
