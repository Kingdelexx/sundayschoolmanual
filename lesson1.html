Lesson 1

SUNDAY JULY 7, 2024

Topic:

Growth Enhancer and Catalyst 1

Bible Texts: Numbers 13:30-33

Welcome to the second half of the year 2024, your year of Exceeding Grace, Growth And Glory.

Thank God for His marvelous acts in your life during the first half of this year. You will without fail experience and accomplish greater things this second half of the year in Jesus mighty name Amen.

Growth Enhancers and Catalysts are factors, things or practices that promotes, foster, boost and fast track our personal growth in desired areas of life. Catalyst of growth refers to something that helps to spur growth, an agent that provokes or speeds significant change. These promoters, enhancers or catalysts are many and varied. We shall be considering a number of them in this study.

1.Positive Mindset: Phil 4:13; Prov. 23:7 Having a positive mental attitude or mindset is to be optimistic about the world around you and expect that good things will happen to you. Constantly expectant of positive outcome in life pursuits and situations at all times.

Steps to developing positive mindset: Turn negative around thoughts into positive thought-Prov. 4:23 Interact within positive environment and with positive people. Go to places that have special meaning and positive memories Make yourself happy-James 1:2-4

Do something that will help others. This will give you a sense of fulfillment and make you feel happy inside-Acts 9:37-39

Embark on whatever you want to do with the conviction or strong persuasion that you will succeed. Finish whatever good thing you start-Heb. 12:2 No one is rewarded for many things he started but the things finished well-2Tim 4:7-8
